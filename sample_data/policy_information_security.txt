INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
Version 5.0 — Internal Policy Document

2. Data Protection

2.1 All personal data of customers is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.

2.4 Access to systems containing customer data is granted based on role and reviewed
quarterly. Access events are logged centrally.

3. Incident Management

3.2 Security incidents must be reported to the internal Security Operations Center
immediately upon discovery. The SOC triages incidents and coordinates response.

4. Awareness

4.1 Employees receive security awareness training during onboarding.
