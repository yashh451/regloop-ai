RECORDS MANAGEMENT POLICY
Version 3.1 — Internal Policy Document

1. Purpose
This policy defines how the organization creates, stores and disposes of business records.

5. Retention Schedules

5.3 Customer transaction records shall be retained for seven years in the corporate
archive system. Records are stored on write-once media to ensure they remain
tamper-evident throughout the retention period.

5.4 Upon expiry of the retention period, records are securely destroyed and a
certificate of destruction is filed.

6. Retrieval
6.1 Archived records can be retrieved by the Records Management team upon written
request. Standard retrieval time is ten business days.
